Format Clarity

XBRL or iXBRL? For Denmark in 2025, the answer is iXBRL.

Pure XBRL with a separate PDF is no longer accepted. Denmark now mandates a single inline XBRL file — one document that is both human-readable and machine-readable.

XBRL + PDF

Pre-2025 · Dual-filing

Historically Danish companies submitted two separate files: an XBRL file with structured data for machines, and a PDF for human readers. This created a risk of inconsistencies between the two.

iXBRL (Inline XBRL)

2025+ · Single file

A single XHTML document with XBRL tags embedded inside. Reads as a normal financial report in a browser and is fully machine-parseable. One source of truth.

Who is affected and when? If your balance sheet date is 31 December 2024 or earlier, XBRL + PDF is still acceptable. If your balance sheet date is 1 January 2025 or later — iXBRL is the only accepted format. ESEF has been iXBRL since FY 2021. Finanstilsynet taxonomy is iXBRL from January 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions

Denmark iXBRL — your questions answered.

Is the filing format XBRL or iXBRL for Denmark?

For every Danish regime from 2025 onwards, the format is iXBRL (inline XBRL) — a single XHTML file with XBRL tags embedded inside. Pure XBRL with a separate PDF is no longer accepted for balance dates from 1 January 2025.

Who must file ESEF iXBRL with Finanstilsynet OAM?

Companies listed on Nasdaq Copenhagen prepare Annual Financial Reports in ESEF under the EU Transparency Directive, enforced by Finanstilsynet. The obligation covers IFRS consolidated financial statements and has applied since FY 2021.

Who must file DK GAAP iXBRL with Erhvervsstyrelsen?

Every commercial company covered by the Danish Financial Statements Act (Årsregnskabsloven) must submit its annual report to Erhvervsstyrelsen in iXBRL. This applies to Class B, C Medium, C Large and D for balance sheet dates from 1 January 2025.

Who must file using the Finanstilsynet taxonomy?

Financial institutions supervised by the Danish FSA — banks, insurers, pension funds, AIFMs, Danmarks Skibskredit A/S and KommuneKredit. Mandatory from January 2025 for in-scope entities.

What about CSRD and Section 99a?

Companies covered by the Danish Financial Statements Act continue reporting on social responsibility under Section 99a until CSRD sustainability reporting phases in. XBRtags supports both Section 99a tagging and CSRD iXBRL as obligations evolve over 2026+.

How does XBRtags compare to software-based solutions?

Software platforms require your team to learn taxonomies, apply tags, fix validation errors and re-tag for taxonomy updates every year. XBRtags is fully managed — you send us the report, we send back a validated iXBRL file.
